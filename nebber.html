Nebber

I was comissionned by Nebber to create some illustrations and scenes for their one-page landing website.
As they wanted to present their product in the form of a story, that could be understood by everyone, we decided to replace men... by animals.

First, he was a man.

Then, he was a slimmy bear...

...and, a big hipster bear

Nebber is a social web app, that helps you to make more things in the "real" world.
In consequence, a lot of characters and environments had to been designed to bring that littel world to live.

Characters

Scenes

A total of eight scenes were designed for that one page.
Each one was there to illustrate some friendly gatherings.
As the website was interactive, and included parallax, some of the scenes were also animated.
